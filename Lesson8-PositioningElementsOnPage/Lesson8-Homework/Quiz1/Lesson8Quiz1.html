Question 1:

If you want to get the width of an element plus the padding, what method would you use?

Answer:

.innerwidth();

Question 2:

Describe in your own words what the position() method returns.

Answer:

the left and top attributes of an element

Question 3:

Explain in your own words the difference between the position() and offset() methods.

Answer:

position() returns the relative left and top attributes of an element, relative to it's parent.

offset() returns the left and top attributes of an alement, relative to the entire document.
